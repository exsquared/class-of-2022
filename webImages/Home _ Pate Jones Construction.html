Community Description

A neighborhood should be a living experience like no other.  That’s why we are excited to introduce Ridgemont Park — a neighborhood within a neighborhood!  Conveniently located off Stadium Boulevard at College Park Drive, Ridgemont Park is a niche of 24 residential building sites in the heart of our vibrant city.  Its central location places you near Mizzou Arena, Faurot Field, University Hospital, shopping and fine dining.  Its proximity to Twin Lakes Recreation Area places you near the beauty and solitude of nature.  Walk out your door and stroll on the paved 2.0 mile County House Trail. Take your four-legged pets to the Twin Lakes dog park to play, or fish in the beautiful lake.  Exercise enthusiasts may want to hike or bike further to access Columbia’s coveted trail system including our State’s MKT trail.  Bike downtown, bike to the University of Missouri, or hike on the trails.  Wherever you go, you’ll want to come home to Ridgemont Park.
