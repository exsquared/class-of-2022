About This Home

Our Gable End Model features 9' ceilings on the first and second floors with an open and exciting floor plan. 42" Kitchen cabinets with crown molding, granite countertops and built-in pantry featuring a classic sliding barn door. Breakfast area leads out to a 10 x 10 Timbertech deck. Spacious En Suite has tray ceiling with a Juliette balcony, luxury bath featuring a 5' shower and double bowl vanity with granite top. Oversized 1-car garage with double parking pad. Finish Recreation room for entertaining.
