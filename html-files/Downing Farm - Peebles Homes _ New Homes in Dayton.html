Downing Farm

summer lawn care and winter snow removal included!

Introducing Downing Farm, an intimate enclave of newly designed craftsman styled bungalow ranch homes located in Washington Township, just west of the intersection of Social Row Road and State Route 48. This convenient location is centrally located with easy access to I-75 and I-675 as well as shopping and dining opportunities in Centerville.

With summer lawn care and winter snow removal included, new single story homes in Centerville starting in the $300s, Downing Farm offers a lifestyle that invites you to stroll through the neighborhood park and visit with your neighbors from your front porch.

Shopping, dining and entertaining couldn’t be any more convenient with Kroger, downtown Centerville, and Austin Landing just minutes away.

Named one of the top Ohio School Districts by Niche.com, families will embrace the stellar educational experience available with the Centerville City School District.

Whether you’re just starting out in home ownership or you’re rightsizing into your perfectly sized home, Downing Farm offers exciting new craftsman styled ranch homes for single level living at its best.

Amenities

Welcome to a new way of living in Downing Farm where you’ll find single level, craftsman styled bungalow homes with covered front porches that encourage you to get outside and meet your neighbors!

At Downing Farm, you’ll enjoy tree lined streets, open spaces, and a 5-acre neighborhood park with future walking trails.

With just 35 single family homes, this intimate enclave of homes includes maintenance free landscaping in the summer and winter snow removal.

Schools

Centerville City Schools embraces a tradition of excellence that dates back to the early 1800s when formal schooling took place in a one-room log cabin. The Centerville-Washington Township community has a long history of demanding quality public schools, with an emphasis on the importance of student accomplishments in academics, athletics and fine arts, stressing the value of a well-rounded education.

Our Favorites

This great Washington Township location is perfectly located. We love how convenient it is for shopping. At State Route 48 and Sheehan Road, you’ll find not only one of the largest Kroger Markets in the area, but a variety of restaurants and shopping as well.

A local favorite has to be Dorothy Lane Market in Springboro.Located less that 10 minutes away on 740 N. Main Street, Dorothy Lane Market is a gourmet grocery shopping experience that you have to do at least once!

Ready to start landscaping, you’ll find everything you need at Grandma’s Garden on State Route 48. A local favorite, Grandma’s Gardens features fountains, ponds and supplies, indoor and outdoor plants, and everything you need to start your own fairy garden.

If outdoor decorating is your passion, The Secret Garden is less than 15 minutes away off Hwy. 73.Here you’ll find a paradise of outdoor yard art, indoor decor, and home goods.
