Saddle Ridge

Are you dreaming of the perfect location to raise a family? Experience life at Saddle Ridge: The fastest-growing residential subdivision within the Cheyenne City limits!

Located in east Cheyenne (just off E. Pershing Blvd. and left on Farthing Road), Saddle Ridge provides convenient access to shopping, restaurants and the Cheyenne Greenway. You can also enjoy nearby parks, walking paths and a neighborhood elementary school.
